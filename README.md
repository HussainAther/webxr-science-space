# 🧪 WebXR Science Space

A modular, spatial research environment built in WebXR — for scientists, educators, and curious minds.  
Walk through data. Spin molecules. View brain scans in 3D. All in your browser.

![screenshot](docs/demo-screenshot.png) <!-- optional image if you add one -->

---

## 🌐 What It Is

**WebXR Science Space** is a 3D, browser-based environment built on A-Frame and Mozilla Hubs-style interactions.  
It’s designed to help researchers and students visualize scientific content *spatially* — not just on a flat screen.

---

## 🔬 Use Cases

| Scene                | What It Does                                                         |
|---------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 🧠 Brain Viewer      | Upload EEG/MRI data as textures on 3D brain models                   |
| 🧪 Molecule Interactor | Drag, rotate, inspect molecular structures like ATP, benzene       |
| 📈 Data Wall         | Upload CSV/JSON and walk through plotted charts in 3D space          |
| 📚 Journal Club      | Pin PDFs to walls, annotate collaboratively with others              |
| 🎥 Lab Demos         | Embed YouTube or Twitch streams of experiments or commentary         |
